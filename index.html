Research

Dissertation

Colonialism and Irish History

Abstract: The conclusions reached by the final dissertation will be threefold: in the first place, the elaboration of a sophisticated Marxist anticolonial theoretical apparatus suited for investigating social phenomena unfolding over many centuries; in the second, the analysis in contour of colonialism in Ireland in terms of that apparatus, a theoretical and factual basis for understanding Irish history in terms of colonialism; in the third place the communication in an implicit form of a particularly (if not uniquely) Irish working-class spirit of critical resistance and scholarship.
